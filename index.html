Vision/Mission Statement:

At La Catrina Café, we want to provide the best service and authenticity of the exotic tastes of Mexico and El Salvador in our sweet and savory pasties, desserts and hot and cold beverages.

Company Summary:

La Catrina Café located in the heart of Prairie Ridge in Bonneylake, WA in sole proprietorship from Larry Joe and Lobby Jange. La Catrina provides you with the authentic and exotic tastes of Mexico and El Salvador in our pastries, desserts and beverages brought to you by Xena and Aracely who were born in Ohio with two immigrant parents deriving from the native plains of Mexico and El Salvador border of Guatemala. Started from an idea of the coffee/café industry lacking variety or culture in its products, we wanted to share our most beloved traditional recipes from our heritage and involve them into the heart of our business.
